About Me

Hi, I'm Brooks Wimer, a Computer Science major at Boston University with a 3.8 GPA, driven by a passion for solving real-world problems through technology. My journey spans from automating emergency notification systems for my internship to building 3D models for fun.

I have a knack for turning concepts into functional solutions, such as the SmartChef Application, a project that integrates recipe discovery with automated grocery delivery using APIs and web drivers. Beyond academics, I've gained hands-on experience as a Software Development Intern at ISO New England, where I contributed to tools for managing the region's power grid.

When I'm not coding, I serve as a Course Assistant for C# full-stack app development at Boston University, play on the Club Roller Hockey Team, and ski whenever I can get the chance. I'm proficient in Python, R, Java, and the C-suite, with growing expertise in SQL, React, and Linux.

I believe in merging creativity with technology, whether it's building comprehensive tools, contributing to the energy sector, or collaborating with teams to create impactful software. I am looking for a role where I can learn a lot about technology and engage in creative problem solving.
