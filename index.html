About Me

Hi, I'm Brooks Wimer, a Software Engineer I at Collette Tours, passionate about building tools that solve real-world problems. My work spans breaking apart legacy systems into modern APIs, building low-latency audio distribution hardware, and designing AI agent orchestration frameworks.

I've built several projects that showcase my technical abilities: SyncSonic — a multi-speaker Bluetooth audio orchestrator for Raspberry Pi; Kitbash — a customizable wireless game controller; Maverick — a custom AI agent orchestration suite for managing multi-project development workflows; and Astra — a cross-platform local network device discovery system.

I studied Computer Science at Boston University (GPA: 3.74), where I served as a Teacher's Assistant for a C# full-stack development course, played on the Club Roller Hockey Team, sat on the Dean's Advisory Board, and was elected as a peer tutor. I'm proficient in C#, Python, TypeScript, Java, and R, with growing expertise in SQL, React Native, and Linux.

I believe in merging creativity with technology to build impactful, durable software.
